Being a Teacher's Assistant requires me to interact with peers and other students on a day to day basis and help explain concepts and techniques to those who don't understand what to do or to help others advance when they have grasped and understood how and when to use different skills. I am in charge of this as well as grading for the class. Quite simply, I am a teacher's assistant.
